“ Where the notes of tradition meet
the melodies of innovation ”

NaadAura is a Society/Organization dedicated to Indian Classical Music. We are a world class centre for excellence in the teaching and propagation of Indian Classical and traditional music and performing arts. We are driven by a modern and contemporary outlook and social sensitivity and are committed to preserving and popularizing the diverse and ancient heritage of India .

Our experience

"NaadAura carries 17 years of experience in organizing musical festivals, workshops, and webinars, promoting Indian classical music and arts, and uplifting youth from marginalized communities."

NaadAura's recognition and legal status

NaadAura is registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860 with Regn No. S/64029/2008. It has received donations exemption under Section 80(G) of the Income Tax Act, 1961 from the Income Tax Department, Government of India. Niti Aayog has also recognized the organization's work and assigned a Unique Id DL/2019/0241999 to NaadAura on its NGO Darpan Portal.

Events at NaadAura

  • Tabla Wizard Tribute

    An event dedicated to the contributions of Tabla Wizard Pandit Anokhe Lal Mishra and Pt. Chhote Lal Mishra, celebrating their remarkable impact on the world of music.

  • Centenary Year Celebration

    Commemorating the centenary of Tabla Maestro Pandit Anokhe Lal Mishra, a cultural heritage from Banaras, with a grand celebration.

  • World Music Day

    A global celebration of music and its universal language, observed annually on June 21st.

  • International Day of Yoga

    Promoting the physical, mental, and spiritual benefits of yoga, celebrated annually from 2015 to 2020.

  • International Indo-Jazz Concert

    An event connecting musical traditions from two nations, fostering cultural exchange and harmony, held in December 2018.

  • Vaisakhi Utsav

    A celebration of semi-classical music and emerging artists, promoting musical talent, observed on May 1, 2019.

  • International Webinar

    Bringing scholars from 20 universities worldwide to discuss strategies for overcoming adversity through the arts, held on March 23rd-24th, 2021.

  • Chakradaar Mahotsav - Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav

    A multi-day celebration in collaboration with UP Sangeet Natak Akademi, marking the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, held in Chitrakoot and Delhi in March 2022.

  • Chakradaar Mahotsav - Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav

    A four-day celebration, supported by the Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India, marking the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav, held in Rishikesh and Delhi in March 2023.
